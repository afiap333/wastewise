About WasteWise


We created WasteWise to draw attention to the problem of overconsumption in the United States, and specifically the issue of fast fashion. We hope that by raising awareness, we can empower people to make more sustainable choices. As Gen Z-ers, we have the opportunity to shape the future to be more eco-friendly. We believe that everyone has the responsibility to do what they can to take care of our planet and ensure its safety for future generations.


This Give Back Project was created as part of the Girls Who Code Leadership Academy. We were part of Group 5, focusing on Game Development. We are grateful for the opportunity to develop our technical skills while creating a project that raises awareness about critical environmental sustainability issues. Thank you to the GWC team!
